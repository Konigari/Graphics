Controls:
1. Movement of red buckets is to the left by 'g' and right by 'h'.
2. Movement of green bucket is to the left by pressing 'j' and right by 'k'
3.The canon moves up and down by the controls 'f' and 's' respectively.
4. The canon angle is increased by 'a' and decreased by 'd'
5.Speed of the falling bricks can be increased by pressing 'n' and decreased by 'm'
6.Pan using keys 7,8,9 and 0.
7. Zoom using + and - keys
8. The canon and brick can also be moved using Mouse.
